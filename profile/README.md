# Welcome to Fluidization Center

欢迎来到流态化联合课题组 Fluidization Center。
本课题组由祝京旭教授创建并领导，致力于流态化及颗粒工程方向的研究。
Fluidization Center 联合了多所高校的师生共同参与，包括：西安大略大学、宁波东方理工大学、广东工业大学、武汉工程大学、宁波诺丁汉大学、中国矿业大学等。

Welcome to the Fluidization Center, a joint research group in fluidization and particle engineering.
The Center was established and is led by Professor Jesse Zhu, and brings together researchers and students from multiple institutions, including Western University, Eastern Institute of Technology, Guangdong University of Technology, Wuhan Institute of Technology, the University of Nottingham Ningbo China, and China University of Mining and Technology.
